Area of Improvement


Improvements could focus on refining feedback quality, enhancing rater training to mitigate biases, and implementing structured follow-up for effective development planning.



Hidden Strengths


Hidden strengths are unrecognized talents or qualities individuals possess, revealed through feedback from peers, supervisors, and subordinates. These strengths, such as strong communication skills or innovative thinking, often go unnoticed in traditional evaluations but can be vital for personal and professional growth when identified and nurtured.



Blind Spots


Blind spots arise from potential biases in feedback, incomplete perspectives from raters, and a lack of contextual understanding. These blind spots can hinder the accuracy and fairness of evaluations, impacting the effectiveness of performance assessments and development initiatives.
